A.Rikka with Nash Equilibrium

Solved by ghk.2:34(+)

  • dp[i][j][k] 放第i个数，放了j行k列，然后从大到小放。
  • dp[i][j][k] * k * (n - j) -> dp[i+1][j+1][k]
  • dp[i][j][k] * j * (m - j) -> dp[i+1][j][k+1]
  • dp[i][j][k] * (j * k - i) -> dp[i+1][j][k]
